What is Every Knee Bow?

Every Knee Bow, is a consecrated space for surrender, intimacy, and the acknowledgment of Christ’s supremacy..

Rooted in Romans 14:11—“Every knee shall bow to Me, and every tongue shall confess to God”—this experience is both a revelation and a response.

IIt is a personal yet corporate experience where hearts are yielded, voices are lifted, and lives are aligned in response to who God is. God's children gathered in one accord to behold Him and respond in worship.

Held periodically, Every Knee Bow brings together worshippers. Each carrying a shared hunger: to minister to the Lord and to dwell in His presence.

Live, Spirit-led worship — unscripted and unfiltered

About Meshakh

Meshack Mulliro, is a devotee of Jesus Christ, serving as a Christian Exegete, Servant-Worship Leader, and a teacher of the Word in formation. His life is anchored in a deep devotion to Christ and a commitment to rightly dividing the Word of God with reverence, clarity, and spiritual depth. He is the Convenor of Every Knee Bow, formerly referred to as Worship Resonance, a worship expression rooted in Romans 14:11 that exists to gather worshippers into sincere, undistracted encounter with God centered on the supremacy of Christ.

Meshakh's interests lie in worship development, music excellence, and spiritual formation within worship teams. He has pursued formal training at Music-Inn International School of Music and RedFouth Music Academy, strengthening his foundation in music and worship leadership. He is particularly focused on integrating musical skill, spiritual sensitivity, and structured team dynamics to cultivate effective worship environments.
Meshack’s ministry conviction is centered on creating sacred spaces where worship, the Word, and life converge. He serves as a Worship Development Coach, working with worship teams on spiritual formation, vocal development, stage coordination, and team alignment.

Beyond ministry, Meshakh operates in the B2B growth and digital systems space, specializing in CRM Consultancy, lead generation, SEO, and web development. He works with businesses to design and implement structured systems that improve customer acquisition, conversion, and retention. His professional focus is on building scalable digital ecosystems that integrate automation, marketing strategy, and customer experience optimization.

Meshakh's vision for Every Knee Bow is to see it become a recurring, transformative worship experience that continually draws worshippers into deeper encounter with God. He envisions it as a catalyst for spiritual awakening, revival, and the strengthening of the church's worship culture in Nairobi and beyond.

He is also the Founder and Lead of SkillForward Youth, an edutech NGO focused on youth development. Through this initiative, he leads a structured programme that integrates digital awareness—understanding how digital life shapes thinking, attention, and behaviour—alongside digital literacy, digital fluency, AI capability, and practical life skills. The programme is designed to equip young Kenyans aged 13–25 to participate meaningfully in the digital economy, blending interpersonal effectiveness with modern technological competence and applied AI skills.

Every Knee Bow is his annual or bi-annual gathering.
